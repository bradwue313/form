"Today we have a very special group here. All of you guys just basically got free tickets to a concert. You might know them as THE Jackson Five, but let them introduce themselves to you."
— ACTION —

Second song

Jidapa solo
"I Want You Back" — The Jackson 5
Reference: Watch on YouTube ↗ · ~2:00
Jidapa
"Now let me tell you about a person at school one day. We met at the sandbox."
Backup singers
*gasp and chatter*
Jidapa
"We toasted our love during morning tea."
Backup singers
"Morning tea?? What are you talking about?"
Jidapa
"I gave him my cookies and my ring pops."

"Awwwww 🥺🥺"
Slot 3
"Who was this person?"

"OOHH! JIDAPA AND… uh.. SITTING IN A TREE! K I S S I N G!"

"[Slot 3]! Ehh, I um.. None of your beeswax."
Jidapa
"So, we fell out during fingerpainting. We were both mad because I didn't play family with him. Then I saw him playing family with another girl!"
Backup singers
*looks shocked, gasps*
Jidapa
"So, one day — that was a Monday — I stepped up to him and said.."
— ACTION —
Intro of "I Want You Back" plays. Jidapa gets back in position. Song begins.

Jidapa + Slot 1

"And I just wanted to say to her.."
